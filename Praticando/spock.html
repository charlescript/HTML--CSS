Leonard Nimoy

as Spock

1931 - 2015

Leonard Nimoy was an actor, author, director, singer and photographer. His most well know role was that of Spock in the Star Trek franchise. The character is a half-vulcan half human star fleet officer. Together with James Kirk and the crew of the USS Enterprise, they explore the far reaches of space. Leonard portrayed Spock from 1964, in Star Trek: The Origina Series to 2013 in the recent reboot film, Star Trek Into Darkness.
